1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us when you fill out a contact form, such as your name, email address, phone number, and practice name.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to respond to your inquiries, provide service information, improve our website, and communicate with you, including via email and SMS/text messages if you have consented.

3. Data Security

We use administrative, technical, and physical security measures to help protect your personal information.

4. SMS/Text Messaging Policy & Terms of Service

This section governs our SMS (text message) communication practices. By providing your consent, you agree to these terms.

A. Obtaining Consent

Your consent to receive SMS messages is obtained through affirmative action, such as checking a designated, non-pre-filled checkbox on our website's contact form. The purpose of the text messages will be clearly stated at the point of consent.

B. SMS Consent and Data Privacy

Information obtained for SMS opt-in consent and phone numbers collected for SMS purposes will NOT be shared with, or sold to, any third parties or affiliates for their marketing purposes.

C. Types of SMS Communications

By opting in, you agree to receive messages which may include: updates regarding your account, service information, appointment reminders, follow-ups to your inquiries, or information about your job application status.

Standard message and data rates may apply. The frequency of messages may vary. You can opt-out at any time.
